# -Sales-Dashboard-using-MS-Excel
Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers to analyze sales performance. It tracks total sales, transactions, average order value, hourly demand, product performance, store-wise sales, category distribution, and weekday trends for data-driven business insights.

# Project Overview

This project is an interactive Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and KPI Cards. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product trends, and store performance. It enables businesses to monitor key metrics and make informed, data-driven decisions.


🎯 Project Objectives 

The dashboard answers the following business questions:


1. How do sales vary by day of the week and hour of the day?
   
• Weekday Sales: Sales remain fairly consistent throughout the week, ranging from 20,510 to 21,701 orders.

• Highest sales day: Friday (21,701 orders).

• Lowest sales day: Saturday (20,510 orders).

• Hourly Sales: Customer orders increase rapidly in the morning, peaking between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM, then gradually decline throughout the afternoon and evening.


2. Are there any peak times for sales activity?

Yes, Peak Hours: 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM, with the highest order quantity around 10:00 AM.

This indicates that customers primarily purchase coffee during morning commute and breakfast hours.


3. What is the total sales revenue for each month?

The coffee shop generated a total sales revenue of $698,812.33 over the six-month period (January–June). Monthly sales show a steady upward trend after February, with June recording the highest revenue and February the lowest.

Key Insights :

• June was the best-performing month, contributing $166,485.88 in sales.

• May ranked second with $156,727.76, indicating strong business growth.

• February had the lowest sales at $76,145.19.

• Sales increased consistently from March through June, suggesting rising customer demand and improved business performance over time.

⏰ Peak Sales Hours :

Customer activity is highest during the morning hours (8 AM – 10 AM), with the maximum order quantity occurring around 10 AM. Sales gradually decline after noon and continue decreasing into the evening.
